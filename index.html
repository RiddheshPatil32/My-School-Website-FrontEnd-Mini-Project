St. Xavier's Education Society
Let Your Light Shine

St. Xavier’s Education Society is a registered society under Society registration Act. 1860 and Bombay Trust Registration Act 1950 approved as a minority organization by the State Government. The Society is based at Mahad of Raigad District established in the year 2002 with various social objectives, prioritized with educational, medical and other social services in the State of Maharashtra. The members of this organization are all Catholics-Christian and all involved in the various social organizations in different parts of Maharashtra. Most among us are practically working as teachers at different places.

Chairman's Message

Johnson P D’souza

St. Xavier’s education society is the only catholic education organization (in Mahad and it’s vicinity) established in Mahad which has a negligible Christian population. We the management of St. Xavier’s education society associated with the intention of catering to the quality education needs of the community. While we were working with different schools in Mahad, there was a constant demand from the local people for a ‘Catholic school ‘, which are often known for their quality and dedicated services. This support and demand of people led St. Xavier’s school to emerge and flourish to the present status.
